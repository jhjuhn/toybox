SPONTANEOUS FORMATION OF PROTOCELL STRUCTURES

A toy model of lipid aggregate exhibiting self-assembly behaviour. The simulation here is modified from the model presented in Edwards, L. (1999), Computational Models for the Formation of Protocell Structures, Doctoral Dissertation; a different sigmoid functions is used, and interactions are optimised with a spatial hash.

  • LMB: exert force on lipids.
  • RMB: add lipids to solution.

J. Lok, July 2017.
