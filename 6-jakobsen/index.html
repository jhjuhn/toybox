JAKOBSEN PARTICLE-CONSTRAINT PHYSICS

A simulation of a system of massive particles and fixed-distance constraints between them. The method, using Verlet integration and iterative constraint relaxation, is developed by Thomas Jakobsen(2001).

This demonstration is implemented in javascript, with HTML/CSS visualisation.

  • SPACEBAR: toggle timstep/edit states
  • CLICK&DRAG: drag particle(timestep state)
  • LEFTCLICK: or remove particle(edit state)
  • RIGHTCLICK: add new particle/constraint(edit state)
J. Lok, November 2016.
